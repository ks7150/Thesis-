symbolism in miniature paintings

Symbolism in Indian miniature paintings serves as a visual language that conveys deeper meanings beyond the surface narrative. Elements like the lotus symbolize spiritual purity and divine presence, while animals such as the peacock represent love and seasonal change, particularly the monsoon.
