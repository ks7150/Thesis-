colors in miniature paintings

Colors carry deep symbolic significance, functioning not only as aesthetic choices but also as vehicles of meaning that reinforce the themes and emotions of each painting. Every hue is deliberately chosen to convey spiritual, cultural, or emotional layers. For instance, blue often represents divinity and cosmic energy (especially in depictions of Krishna), while red signifies passion, power, or sacred rituals. Yellow denotes learning and auspiciousness, often associated with saints or seasons like spring, and green evokes nature, prosperity, and harmony. These colors do more than decorate; they guide the viewer's interpretation, anchoring the narrative in cultural codes and emotional resonance.
